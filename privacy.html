Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2025

Thank you for using tasktrade! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Tasktrade app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name and Email Address: Collected through Sign in with Apple when you create an account.
  • Task Content: The tasks ("todos") you create and trade with others in the app. These are visible to all users of Tasktrade.
  • App Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics to understand how users interact with the app and improve our features.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your Tasktrade account
  • Display your shared tasks to other users
  • Analyze app usage to improve the user experience
  • Communicate with you only if absolutely necessary

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Your tasks may be visible to other users, but your email is never shared publicly. Analytics data is used internally and may be aggregated to improve app performance.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no method of internet transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Choices

You can request to delete your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.
